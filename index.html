Crisostomo Ibarra A kind young man. He is the main character of Noli Me Tangere. His love interest is Maria Clara.
Maria Clara A very beautiful woman. She is the love interest of Crisostomo Ibarra.
Padre Damaso A greedy friar. One of the antagonists in Noli Me Tangere.
Kapitan Tiago A very wealthy man. His real name is Don Santiago de los Santos. He hosted a party in the first chapter to welcome Crisostomo Ibarra.
Don Rafael Ibarra He is the deceased father of Crisostomo Ibarra. He was also a very wealthy man, and some were jealous of his wealth.
